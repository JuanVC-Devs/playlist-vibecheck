# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant, version 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

We pledge to make participation in this community a harassment-free experience for everyone. Be respectful of differing opinions, give and accept constructive feedback gracefully, and focus on what is best for the community. Harassment, trolling, derogatory comments, and publishing others' private information are unacceptable.

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported privately to the maintainer ([@JuanVC-Devs](https://github.com/JuanVC-Devs)). All complaints will be reviewed promptly and fairly, and the reporter's privacy will be respected. Consequences range from a warning to a permanent ban.

For the full text see the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).
